2. Description of Service

LinzNext is an application that displays real-time departure information for public transit in Linz, Austria. The app is available for iOS, watchOS, and macOS and uses your location to identify nearby transit stops.

The App operates in two modes:

  • Free: Displays the nearest transit stop
  • Premium: Displays all nearby stops (within approximately 500 meters)
