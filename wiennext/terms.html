2. Description of the service

WienNext is an application that displays real-time departure information for public transport in Vienna, Austria. The app is available for iOS and Android and uses the user's location to identify nearby stops.

The app works in two modes:

  • Free: Shows the nearest stop
  • Premium: Shows all nearby stops (within a radius of about 500 metres) and offers a stop search with map for all of Vienna

WienNext is an unofficial, independent app with no affiliation to Wiener Linien or the City of Vienna.
